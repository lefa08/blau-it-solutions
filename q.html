FAQ

Here are some answers to some common questions.

How much does a website cost?

Our starting price is R899, for one-page websites. For e-Commerce websites, and the minimum investment is R2999+ Depanding on Number of Pages

What are your payment terms for web development?

Payment is split into three parts:
- 50% upfront
- 50% When website is launched

How long will my website project take?

Depending on the size of the project and the time allocated to collect content, information and feedback, projects take 5 to 10 working days to be completed.

Do you offer maintenance/ongoing support packages?

Always. Please get in touch with us.

Yes, but we always ask our client if they want to keep their current provider or swap to a new one.

If you are interested in web hosting services, please contact us.
